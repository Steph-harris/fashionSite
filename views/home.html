About the Designer

I am a colorful, extroverted designer with a deep love for marine life. Before graduating from Centenary College in 2009, I interned with high end designers in New York City such as Michael Kors and Giorgio Armani. Shortly after, I held an assistant position at Jones New York until its closure in 2015. I currently work at Calvin Klein as an Associate Colorist.

Foundation Partnership

After years in the industry, I've realized my knowledge can be more impactful if utilized in the right way. I am launching a line of clothes that will be connected to the conservation and preservation of sea animals. I want to give back to the researchers that dedicate their time and bring awareness to our slowly dying ecosystem. Once I am closer to the release of the line, I will be giving some of the proceeds to the appropriate foundation.

Please stay tuned for the launch of a truly influential line of clothes!
